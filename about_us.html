About Play Vault

Play Vault is a free online game hub with 20+ addictive puzzle, arcade, and action games — from block puzzles and match-3 games to space shooters and endless runners. No downloads, no signups, no timers. Just pick a game and start playing instantly in your browser.

Our Mission

We build simple, satisfying games that anyone can pick up in seconds and enjoy for hours. Every game on Play Vault is completely free to play, and the site is supported by ads shown through our advertising partner, Adsterra.
